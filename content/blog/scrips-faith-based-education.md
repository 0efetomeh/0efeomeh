# SCRIPS: a decentralized school management system for faith communities

## Built for communities, not just institutions

SCRIPS is designed for faith-based schools that need private, values-aligned tools they can actually own and manage. It keeps the workflow close to the community instead of pulling it into a generic central platform.

## Core priorities

- Privacy-first records and reporting that respect sensitive information.
- Offline-ready operation so work can continue even when networks are unreliable.
- Decentralized storage that keeps control in the hands of the community.
- Clear reporting and conflict handling for multi-user collaboration.

## Why the trade-offs are worth it

A decentralized model asks for more structure up front, but it pays back in autonomy, trust, and resilience. The system feels slower to design and much easier to own.
